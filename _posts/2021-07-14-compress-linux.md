There are several commands provided by linux to unzip a compressed file. 
The choice of either is however dependent on the file name. The syntax is as follows:

<br>

## Zip 

`
unzip filename.zip
`

<br>

## Tar 

`
tar xvf filename.tar
`

<br>

## Gunzip

`
gunzip filename.gz
`
